Re: The wonderful rabbit hole of BBEdit

Posted on October 15, 2024 by Loren

I've used the free version of BBEdit starting back when the free version was TextWrangler. The only thing I used it for up to now was for the occasional time that I needed to write something in a plain text editor. When I decided to build a blog using html and css, I decided to pay for the full version of BBEdit.

Inspired by Nicolas Magand, now that I own the full version of BBEdit, I figured I should use it for writing markdown blog posts. Most markdown editors have keyboard shortcuts for bold, italic, and links, but not BBEdit. Since Nicolas switched from MarkEdit to BBEdit I figured he must have figured something out for this. And, of course, he had:
